Our Story

A Cafe Made for Comfort

✿

La Vie

Beautiful coffee moments, made daily

Cafe La Vie brings together the charm of a Parisian cafe with the ease of a neighborhood coffee shop. It is a place for fresh pastries, thoughtful espresso, and a comfortable seat in a beautiful room.

Our espresso is pulled with precision, our pastries are baked fresh each morning, and our coffee is roasted in-house by Skyview Roastery.

The idea is simple: bring the charm of a Parisian cafe into a space that feels relaxed, familiar, and comfortable enough for every day.
